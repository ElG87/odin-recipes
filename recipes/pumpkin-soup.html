Pumpkin Soup

Pumpkin Soup submitted by Allrecipes

This pumpkin soup is a creamy and comforting dish that is perfect for fall. It's made with fresh pumpkin, onions, garlic, and a blend of spices that create a rich and savory flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium pumpkin, peeled and cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup heavy cream (optional)

Steps

  1. Step 1:

    In a large pot, saut√© the chopped onion and minced garlic until translucent.

  2. Step 2:

    Add the cubed pumpkin and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the pumpkin is tender.

  3. Step 3:

    Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth. If you don't have an immersion blender, you can carefully transfer the soup to a regular blender in batches.

  4. Step 4:

    Stir in the ground ginger, ground cinnamon, salt, and pepper. If desired, add heavy cream for a richer flavor.

  5. Step 5:

    Serve hot, garnished with a sprinkle of cinnamon or a drizzle of cream if desired.

    6. Enjoy your delicious pumpkin soup!
