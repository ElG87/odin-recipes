Chili con Carne

Chili con carne submitted by Allrecipes

This chili con carne is a hearty and flavorful dish that is perfect for a cozy night in.

It's made with ground beef, beans, and a blend of spices that create a rich and savory flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (15 ounces) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Step 1:

    In a large pot, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.

  2. Step 2:

    Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pot. Cook until the onion is translucent.

  3. Step 3:

    Add the kidney beans, diced tomatoes, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.

  4. Step 4:

    Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  5. Step 5:

    Serve hot, garnished with cheese or sour cream if desired.

    6. Enjoy your delicious chili con carne!
